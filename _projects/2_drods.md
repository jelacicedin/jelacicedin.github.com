## A Digital Twin Framework for Dynamic and Robust Distributed Systems (D-RODS)

A collaborative research initiative developing an autonomous system testing and operational framework centered on digital twin technology, artificial intelligence, and validation methodologies.

**Status:** Active (May 2023 – May 2026)

**Key Focus:**
- Developing a framework for highly autonomous system testing and operation
- Integrating digital twins with AI and formal verification approaches
- Optimizing resource utilization and increasing system resilience to faults
- Supporting circular economy principles and operational efficiency

**Core Technologies:**
- Digital twin technology
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning
- Verification and validation methodologies
- Continuous learning and model verification

**Application Domains:**
- Manufacturing
- Transportation
- Telecommunications

**Industrial Partners:**
- ABB Corporate Research
- Alstom
- Ericsson AB

**Institutional Base:**
Departments of Intelligent Future Technologies and Networked and Embedded Systems at Mälardalen University

For more information, visit the [D-RODS project page](https://www.es.mdu.se/projects/615-A_Digital_Twin_Framework_for_Dynamic_and_Robust_Distributed_Systems_).
